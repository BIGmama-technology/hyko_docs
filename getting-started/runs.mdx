---
title: Blueprint Runs
description: 'Explore and manage your blueprint execution history'
---

Hyko.ai allows you to review and manage your previous blueprint runs :

1. **Restore Previous Runs**: Easily revert to a previous state or recreate specific outputs.
2. **View Results**: Access and analyze the previous outputs.
3. **Cost Analysis**: Track the expenses associated with each run.
4. **Status Monitoring**: Check the current state of your runs, whether they're completed, in progress, or encountered issues.
5. **Performance Metrics**: Evaluate the execution time of your blueprints.
